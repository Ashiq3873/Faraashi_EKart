Frequently Asked Questions

How do I trace my delivery?

You can use the Track Order tab right next to this section. Simply sign in, select the order from your history, and view the live shipment stage updated directly by our fulfillment team.

Can I pay on delivery?

Yes, we offer Cash on Delivery (COD) across all pincodes within India at no additional processing fees. Simply choose COD at checkout.

How long does order fulfillment take?

We process all orders within 24 hours. The standard delivery window is 3-5 business days depending on your postal address.

Returns & Refund Policy

We believe in honest services. If you aren't completely satisfied with your order, we make returns straightforward.

30-Day Money-Back Guarantee

No questions asked return on any unused item in its original brand box/condition.

How to initiate a return:

  1. Keep the product packaging intact, containing tags and bills.
  2. Send a direct return request email to mohamedashiqb12@gmail.com with your Order ID.
  3. Our support team will coordinate a pickup within 48 hours.
  4. Refunds are processed back to your bank account or payment source within 5 business days after inspection.

Non-Returnable Items

Perishable food items, custom cosmetics, and inner garments cannot be returned due to hygiene and health regulations. Please review individual item details before checkout.
